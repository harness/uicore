<Meta title="Foundation/Intent" />

Intent is used as a decoration on top of components. It adds universal styles (color, background color, etc...) into the components it decorates.

Five intents are defined currently.

- `Intent.NONE`, or `none`: No intent or neutral intent.
- `Intent.PRIMARY`, or `primary`: Primary.
- `Intent.SUCCESS` or `success`: Success.
- `Intent.WARNING`, or `warning`: Warning.
- `Intent.DANGER`, or `danger`: Danger.

When applying an intent to a text components like [Heading](/heading) or [Text](/text), its color changes to reflect the intent.

```js

<Heading intent="primary">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading intent="success">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading intent="warning">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Heading intent="danger">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Heading>
<Text intent="primary">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Text>
<Text intent="success">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Text>
<Text intent="warning">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Text>
<Text intent="danger">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog</Text>

```
